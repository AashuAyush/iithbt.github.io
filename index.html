About Us

We're the Official Inter-IIT Magazine

IIT HeartBeat

The magazine focuses on the subjects involving the IIT's i.e. their fests, the latest developments taking place, the concerns of the students and the daily happenings taking place around them. It looks at the situation from their eyes and includes whatever the general body feels in the articles involving them. Apart from that, it also covers interviews of the alumni, profs etc.The group has the sole objective of establishing its roots in IIT Roorkee and gradually building it in other IIT's as well. In this way, the information between the IIT's can pass both quickly and effectively. It aims at making all the IITIAN's aware of the events occurring across other IIT's, so that they do not miss out on any activity. Moreover, it also transfers the invaluable message that a prof/alumni has to give to the students which would help them in their future experiences.
